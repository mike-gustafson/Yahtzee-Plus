How to Play Yahtzee

Objective

Score the most points by rolling five dice to make specific combinations over multiple turns.

Game Rules

  • On each turn, you can roll the dice up to three times.
  • After each roll, you can click on individual dice to "hold" them, keeping their values for the next roll.
  • Once you are satisfied with your dice roll, select a scoring category on the scorecard.
  • Each category can only be used once per game.
  • When both the Upper and Lower scorecard sections are completed, the game ends.

Scoring

Score points based on specific combinations, divided into Upper and Lower sections:

  • Upper Section: Score points based on dice showing numbers 1 to 6. For example, if you choose "Threes," you score the sum of all dice showing 3.
  • Lower Section: Score points for combinations such as Three of a Kind, Full House, Small Straight, Large Straight, Yahtzee (five of the same number), and Chance.

How to Play

  • Click "Roll" to start your turn.
  • Click on dice to hold them for the next roll, or roll again to try for a better combination.
  • When you're ready, click on a scoring category in the scorecard to save your score and end the turn.
  • Click "Next Turn" to begin a new turn.
  • Once all categories are filled, the game will display "Game Over!"

Controls

  • Roll: Starts a roll or re-roll of the dice.
  • Next Turn: Advances to the next turn after scoring.
  • New Game: Resets the game to start over.
